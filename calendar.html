How to add

To use the calendar you have a few options:

Bookmark & view only

This is the simplest approach. You simply bookmark the troop website or the calendar directly. This doesn't require any accounts or special magic.

  • http://troop128.net - Please note that this view combines both Troop & Venture Crew into one (see below).
  • Troop128Milford calendar
  • Crew128Milford calendar

Add to your Google calendar

This requires a Google account. The benefit is that you'll see the Troop and/or Crew calendars in your personal calendar for one stop viewing.

To add a Google calendar, click on the direct Google calendar links above. You'll find the "+ Google Calendar" link in the lower right. If you click on this, it'll add it to your personal Google calendars.

If you have an iOS device (iPhone, iPad, iPod), you may need to tell visit the following site from your device's browser to enable synchronizing the calendar to your device. Here's the link:
https://calendar.google.com/calendar/syncselect
