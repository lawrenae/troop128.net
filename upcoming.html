Au Sable River (July 8-13)

We are heading out for a canoe trip to Grayling Michigan where we will canoe, fish, swim, eat, sleep, and have a wonderful time on the beautiful Au Sable River. If you are interested in going on this event, we need commitments by the troop meeting on March 13th. The cost of this trip is $175. A $25 deposit is required by March 13th to hold your spot. The second payment of $75 is due April 17th. The third payment of $75 is due May 15th. Below is the basic itinerary of the trip.

Details:

  • July 8th - Leave early morning to arrive in Dearborn Michigan.
  • July 8th - We will visit the Henry Ford Museum and camp at Camp Munhacke
  • July 9th - We will drive to Grayling Michigan and camp at Hartwick Pines. We will arrive at camp midday and plan on doing some day/afternoon evening hiking.
  • July 10th - We will take a short drive to Penrods Canoe Rental, get our canoes and hit the river! We will camp the night at White Pine.
  • July 11th - We continue our trip on the Au Sable River, camping at Parmalee.
  • July 12th - We get in the canoes and finish out 49th mile on the river, ending at Mio Pond. We will camp at Mio Dam campsite.
  • July 13th - We drive back to Cincinnati.

Costs: The cost of the trip will be $175. There are will be 2 meals that need purchased by the scouts, 1 on the way and 1 on the way back.

Additional Requirements to attend this trip:

  1. All scouts will need to pass the swimmers test in June.
  2. There will be a morning at Camp Friedlander in May where we will witness and make sure that everyone has the required abilities to maneuver the canoes. (required)
  3. We will also have a 1 day local canoe trip (required).
  4. All first year scouts will be required to have a parent with them.
  5. We will not be transporting personal canoes or kayaks. We will most likely rent 1 or 2 kayaks for the scouts to try. We will be transporting all of our gear down the river. Everything will need to be packaged in dry bags. I have posted a few links to help everyone familiarize yourself with the trip.

Important Dates:

  • March 13th - registration - deposit of $25 due
  • April 17th - 2nd payment of $75 due.
  • May 15th - 3rd final payment $75 due.
  • Mid May - TBD - canoe day at Camp Friedlander
  • Mid June - TBD - canoe a local river
  • Mid June - gear / trip review
  • Late June - shake down
  • July 8th - Leave
  • July 13th - Return

This is a very technical canoe trip, but it is very beautiful! I have personally been on this trip and can't wait to go again!
