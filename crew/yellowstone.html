Packing List (PDF Reference)

  • backpack (65 liter or more)
  • hiking poles
  • Sleeping gear:
    • Sleeping bag (rated to 20-40 deg F)
    • sleeping pad or air mattress, insulated
    • bag liner (optional)
    • pillow (optional)
  • clothes:
    • rain gear
    • jacket
    • bandanna, buff and/or hat
    • hiking boots
    • sandals
  • eating gear:
    • 2-3 liter water bladder
    • 1-2 liter water bottle
    • folding bowl (fossil type easy to clean & store)
    • spork (light my fire backpacking)
    • cup
  • personal first aid kit:
    • foot powder
    • bandaids
    • moleskin(consider sharing)
  • toiletries: toothbrush, toothpaste, floss, comb, camp towel
  • duffle bag (for non-trek clothes & gear)
  • mesh bag, stuff sacks, ziplock bags
  • required: LED flashlight/headlamp
  • optional/shared: camera w/ case, compass, watch, whistle, extra batteries, notepad/pen
