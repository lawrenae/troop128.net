Feast of the Harvest Moon (Lafayette, IN) (Spet 29-Oct 1)

More information:
  • http://feastofthehuntersmoon.org/
  • http://carycamp.com/
  • http://www.homeofpurdue.com/events/events/feastofthehuntersmoon.html

The weekend of September 29- October 1st we will be traveling to West Lafayette, Indiana to visit the Feast of the Hunters' Moon. Cost for this trip is $35.00 per person. This is a great trip for parents too!

Anyone going on this trip needs to have a copy of their Annual Medical Record form Part A and B on file with the Crew. Anyone over 18 must complete youth protection training and have your information on file with the crew. If you are not sure you need to ask.

We are leaving on Friday the 29th and driving to Franklin L Cary Camp in Layette Indiana. Click on the link for more information about the camp we will be staying.

Address of the Camp:
Franklin L Cary Camp
6286 State Road 26 East
Lafayette, IN 47905

We are staying here Friday night and Saturday night.

Our schedule will be:
  • Friday, September 29th - meet at church 5:30pm (eat dinner before arriving at church). Leave at 6pm. We'll be setting up tents in the dark, so be sure to have a head lamp / flashlight handy.
  • Saturday, September 30th
    • Quick and non-cooking breakfast and Crew cooking for dinner
    • Crew will buy their own meal for lunch at the festival (bring money).
    • Spend most of the day at the Feast of the Hunters' Moon
  • Sunday, Oct. 1
    • Crew non-cooking breakfast
    • Return to church about 1:00

Visit this web site for more information on the Feast. At this time the 2017 schedule of events is not posted, but you can see what happened in 2016.
