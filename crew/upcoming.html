Blue Ridge Scout Reservation, VA (June 11-17)

Join us this summer for the adventure of a life time! We will be heading to the Blue Ridge Scout Reservation at Camp Powhatan in Hiwassee, VA for five days of high adventure fun. You can choose either a 5 day Voyageur Adventure Program which includes a five day whitewater canoeing/rafting trip on the beautiful New River or a five day New River Adventure program which includes COPE, natural rock climbing, rafting, ATV riding, caving and cowboy action shooting.

The Voyageur Program will provide white water canoeing over 50 miles in four days. Camping will be spending on the river and a support vehicle will shadow the trek moving gear to each day’s overnight camp area. After a quick trip down the river on Thursday, your group will be transported to West Virginia’s New river Gorge for your overnight stay. Friday will be spent rafting the New River Gorge, later in the day your group will be transported back to Camp Powhatan for your final overnight.

The New River Adventure will start after breakfast each day, your group will select each day’s activities from a list of high adventure to include COPE, Ice Climbing, Natural Rock climbing, Rafting/Canoeing, ATV riding, Caving and Cowboy Action Shooting. This will be a summer camping type setting where you will be sleeping in the same tent each night and eating breakfast and dinners at the dining hall each morning and night. The group will be transported by the camp staff to any activities conducted outside the camp.
